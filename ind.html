Welcome to the official County Budget Revenue and Allocation Management System. Our main goal is to create an automated, user-friendly and seamless way of paying our taxes online and automating the procurement process towards the delivery of goods and services that in the long run benefits the taxpayer. It was important to automate the entire system so as to seal all the gaps by which public funds are embezzled by a few corrupt government officials. This website also seeks to eliminate the long cues witnessed when filing for our taxes at the tax offices. In conclusion: LET US PAY OUR TAXES, LET US BUILD OUR COUNTRY!
